facts section

Fashion is the number two polluting industry in the world, second only to the oil industry.
source: forbes

Only one out of every ten items of clothing donated are resold. The rest ends up in landfills or developing countries.

tips

  • Value and take good care of the clothing you already own.
  • Shop less, choose better. Only buy pieces you love.
  • Go for clothes that are high quality.
  • Buy vintage and secondhand clothing when possible.
  • Support ethical brands.
  • Try recycling your used textiles. Find a local textile waste bin near you.
